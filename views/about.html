About Me

Hi, I’m Chris Campbell, and I recently left a career in Commercial Real Estate. I’ve always been entrepreneurially spirited, and thrive off of creativity. While grateful for my experience gained in the field, I found that these intrinsic values were left unfulfilled in my past career. I came to the realization that I could solve problems, and create beautiful apps at the same time as a web developer. I tried learning on my own, but realized I needed more formal training, so I enrolled in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. The program is very rigorous and I’ve developed proficiency in html5, CSS, Javascript and Node.js, among other languages. I look forward to building simple, beautiful, user-centered apps!
